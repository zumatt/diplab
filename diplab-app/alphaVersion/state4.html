DiPLab

Thanks to the Antibiotics, now the petri dish is ready to be analyzed. You will have two different modality: history and microscope mode.

In history mode you will have the possibility to see the evolution of the DiPLab Petri in 4 different steps: 0 hours, 8h, 12h and 24h. These steps come from the typical analysis of Petri dishes in the labs; 8h is when antibiotics start to grow, 12h is the first antibiogram test and 24h is the second one where scientists check the resistance.
